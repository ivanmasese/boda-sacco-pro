❓ Frequently Asked Questions
How do I increase my loan limit? ▼
Save consistently every week. Your loan limit is 3× your total savings. The more you save, the more you can borrow.
How long does loan approval take? ▼
Loan requests are reviewed by your SACCO admin. This usually takes 1–2 business days. You will receive an SMS notification when approved.
What happens if I miss a loan payment? ▼
A late penalty will be added to your balance. Contact your SACCO admin immediately if you cannot make a payment.
How do I change my PIN? ▼
Visit your SACCO office with your National ID to request a PIN change. Your admin will reset it for you.
Is my money safe in this system? ▼
Yes. All transactions are recorded and you can download receipts for every transaction. Your savings are held by your SACCO.
